Upload your personal documents to ArabicReader and read them online. For instance, students can upload their coursework and help each other by adding to the database any words that are unknown to the system. Your fellow students can then upload and read the same text and will not need to spend time looking up words in the dictionary, and can instead focus on memorising the vocabulary and studying the grammar.

Also, you can upload essays and books (subject to copyright of course) and read them online with the full facilities of the one-click online dictionary functionality.

Please enter the title of this document. You will be able to choose this document by name in the Read section of the site, in the My Library area.
N.B. The file must be a text file in utf-8 encoding. Microsoft Word documents can be converted to this format by going File/Save As..., choosing 'Plain Text (*.txt)', and then select Unicode (UTF-8) from the Other encoding radio button.
