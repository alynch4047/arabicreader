Torque Geometric

Torque Geometric is a live wallpaper for Android phones.

  1. It offers an attractive `live' geometric pattern based on Islamic patterns (zillij)
  2. It has fully configurable color schemes
  3. Authentic Islamic patterns are rendered dynamically using mathematical algorithms

How To Use

After downloading and installing the software, you need to active Torque Geometric as your preferred wallpaper. To do so, hold your finger down on your phones home screen, and a choice should come up offering wallpaper, live wallpaper or. Choose Live Wallpaper, and then select Torque Geometric from the list. To use with the default settings, simply press 'Set Wallpaper' at this point. To change the level of detail, colors etc., then return to this screen and choose Settings, before again choosing Set Wallpaper.
